Authentic Pizza Margherita

Discription

This Margherita pizza is topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, olive oil, and a little bit of sea salt, then baked on a pizza stone. Sometimes simple is best. Reviewers recommend making your own pizza sauce, or using high-quality store-bought, for the best results.

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • ¼ cup flour for dusting
  • 2 cups pizza sauce
  • 20 slices fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 20 leaves fresh basil
  • olive oil
  • sea salt to taste

Steps

  1. Stir flour and 1 teaspoon salt in a bowl. Set aside.
  2. Mix water, yeast, and sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Stir half the flour mixture into yeast mixture until no dry spots remain.
  4. Lightly oil a large bowl, then place dough in the bowl and turn to coat with oil.
  5. Stretch out and pat 1 dough ball to form a circle 10 to 12 inches in diameter.
  6. Slide each pizza onto the pizza stone in the preheated oven.
